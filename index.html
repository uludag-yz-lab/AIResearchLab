Research Areas

DEVOps

We integrate modern DevOps practices into AI processes to ensure the sustainability and scalability of our projects.

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Designing scalable learning algorithms — from representation learning to large-scale neural architectures and efficient fine-tuning.

Wireless Networks

Graph-based methods for knowledge representation and inference over multi-type, multi-relational network structures.

MLOps

Building end-to-end machine learning pipelines with automated monitoring, versioning, and deployment for reproducible, production-ready AI systems.

About This Research

This study presents a comprehensive MLOps-driven framework for predicting ocean oxygen saturation levels using machine learning. The research addresses the critical need for accurate and automated environmental monitoring systems in marine ecosystems.

By integrating MLOps principles — including automated data pipelines, continuous model training, and deployment workflows — the proposed system ensures reproducibility, scalability, and practical applicability in real-world oceanographic contexts.
